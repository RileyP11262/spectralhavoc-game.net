Locations Announced!

Get ready to explore the diverse and thrilling locations of Spectral Havoc.

  • Benevolent Blister: Violent waves reside in the sea, wonder what resides in these waves...
  • Celestial City: A city by the gods, angels live here.
  • Deep Depths: Gigantic pits in the sea, unagis live here.
  • Exotic Elevations: Happy mountains with Japanese vibes.
  • Feta Flotilla: Ship that sails around to transport in and out, of Eldoria, the mystical wonders of the edge of the world.
  • Hacienda Heights: Spanish city with festive lights and accomodations, you can be immersed into Spanish culture in your house.
  • Hollow Hamlet: A ghost town that no longer have any residents, you can occupy it to protect it from a rat infestation.
  • Interior Interstice: Inside a volcano, a city that beams the light and carries through the pain.
  • Maize Minaret: All you see are corn fields for miles, full of foliage and livelyness.
  • Numa's Nether: Monsters protect an ancient town, cursed with 1,000 years of trouble by Numa.
  • Placid Plateaus: Peaceful islands help keep the vibe, and of course, you can drink pineapple juice on a palm tree.
  • Rubbish Remains: Remains after a volcano erupted 100 years ago, all you see are residents that are afraid of the AI apocalypse
  • Sunken Sanctuary: A temple in which resides ancient treasure, explore and you will find it with the 7 cues.
  • Viper Vertex: Dragons live here to protect the ancient tribes of the China Empire.

The journey awaits!
